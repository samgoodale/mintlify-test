## When does the SDK send a tracking request for analytics?

A tracking request is typically sent when you explicitly call a tracking method such as `trackConversion()` or `flush()`, or when a method is documented as emitting exposure or analytics data as part of its behavior. The exact triggers depend on the SDK and method being used.
