Use visit history and stored visitor data to enrich targeting decisions across feature flags and experiments.

## Handling data in Kameleoon SDKs

When SDKs retrieve previously stored visitor data, that data can be used for targeting, reporting, and maintaining continuity across visits and devices, depending on the SDK and the data scope that was configured.
