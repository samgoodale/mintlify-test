---
title: "Cross-Device Experimentation"
description: "Concepts for linking visitor activity across devices and sessions."
---

Cross-device experimentation lets you associate activity from the same user across multiple devices or sessions so evaluation and reporting can remain consistent.

## Activating cross-device history reconciliation

Use a stable unique identifier and ensure the relevant custom data is configured with the appropriate scope so linked visits can be reconciled.

## Critical points and practical insights

Keep identifier quality, data scope, and synchronization timing consistent across devices so merged histories behave predictably in targeting and reporting.
