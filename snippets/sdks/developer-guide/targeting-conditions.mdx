The Kameleoon SDKs support a variety of predefined targeting conditions that you can use to target users in your campaigns. For the list of conditions this SDK supports, see [use visit history to target users](https://developers.kameleoon.com/feature-management-and-experimentation/using-visit-history-in-feature-flags-and-experiments).

You can also use your own [external data to target users](https://developers.kameleoon.com/feature-management-and-experimentation/use-external-data-to-target-users).
